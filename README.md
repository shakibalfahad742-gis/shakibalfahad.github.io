# Md Shakib Al Fahad
I work with geospatial data to understand environmental problems, especially hazards like flooding and landslides, and how climate influences vegetation patterns.

Along with geospatial analysis, I have field-based experience through land use surveys, geomorphological observations, and hydrological assessments. This helps me connect spatial data with real-world environmental conditions instead of relying only on remote analysis. 

My work combines satellite data, GIS modeling, and field-based understanding. I have worked on real projects with government agencies and NGOs in Bangladesh, not just classroom exercises.
